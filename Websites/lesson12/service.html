Services

Laundry Services:

Some temples allow for the rental of temple clothing for baptisms, initatories, and endowment sessions. Some temples offer this service while others do not. Information on which temples offer this service can be found listed on the temples page. We offer laundry services to ensure your temple clothing is as fresh and clean as the temple youre visiting!

Cafeteria:

Food services are offered in some temples where various meals are provided. We also offer our own food services alogn with breakfast lunch and dinner served at 6 hour intervals

Pool and exercise rooms:

Every one of our hotels offers Pools and rooms fitness rooms. These services are included with your reservation.
