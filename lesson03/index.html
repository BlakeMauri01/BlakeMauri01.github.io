Site Name:

Idahoan Inclement

Purpose:

This site is designed to display the local weather report for various towns and cities in Idaho. A five day forcast, weather article, map, and contact information will be present on the home page of the site. Various town names in idaho will be availible for selection which will display the local weather forcast of the selected town.
